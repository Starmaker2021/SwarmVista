SwarmVista

SwarmVista integrates visualization of swarm behaviours in natural environments. You can adjust model parameters, interact directly with the models, and explore the fun of swarm behaviours!

How do I access the model?

Click the "Try Now" button above, or select a model from the tab bar at the top of the page to enter the model page.

How do I operate the model?

Adjust the model's settings in the parameter panel, and the model will immediately visualize according to the new configuration. Alternatively, you can interact directly with the model on the visualization panel using your mouse.

Tips and Tricks

Detailed text and video introduction

On the left sidebar of the interface, you can read a text introduction of the model, watch videos, and access links to related literature on the model.

High-performance visualization supported by D3.js

The models on the website are visualized using D3.js, offering high flexibility. Data binding ensures intuitive and effective data updates and synchronization.

Interactive visualization panel

Most models incorporate mouse events, allowing you to experiment by clicking or double-clicking elements within the visualization panel to observe changes in the model.

Reasonable initial configuration

The model loads with default parameter settings, allowing for smooth model operation without the need for initial setup.

Comprehensive control panel operations

In the control panel, you can adjust the model's visualization effects, modify the settings of the visualization panel, and alter the model's parameters.

Explanation for complex parameters

Beside parameters that might be challenging to understand, there often are explanations, making it user-friendly for non-experts.
