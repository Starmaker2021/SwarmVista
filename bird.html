Birds Model

Model Introduction

While similar in concept to the Boids model, the Bird model focuses more on the nuances of bird flight. Birds use up energy in flight, and when they are low on energy, they stop at a nearby tree to regain it. And it perfectly captures the essence of birds flying together, creating mesmerizing patterns in the sky.
Drag trees to change the weather in the forest and observe the behavior of the birds in your defined forest!
