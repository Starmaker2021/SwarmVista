Predator-Prey Model

This model captures predator-prey interactions. In this model, the shark represents the predator and the prey is represented by common fish; the shark rushes towards the fish within its field of view, and the prey hug more tightly as the shark approaches. This represents the predator-prey relationship in an ecological context.
