Fish model

The fish model mimics the schooling behavior of fish, showing how individual aquatic organisms use local interactions to move as a whole. Each fish, gauging its surroundings, adjusts its speed and direction to its neighbours. This creates fascinating formations, such as schools of fish gracefully weaving and turning through complex environments, and it all starts with simple guiding principles.
Move rocks, set the direction and speed of the waterflow, and let the fish move through the river you create!
