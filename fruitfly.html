FruitFly Model

The model delves into the microcosm of Drosophila, examining their behavioral patterns. Drosophila lock onto the location of fruits by optics and odor and keep moving closer to the optimal solution. The determinants of the optimal solution are the distance from the fruit and the degree of decay of the fruit, respectively.
Drag the fruit or click on it to make it more rotten, and watch for changes in the fruit fly's movement!
