FireFly Model

Fireflies, with their synchronized lighting, have long fascinated observers. The Firefly model tries to decode this phenomenon. Each firefly, driven by intrinsic timers and influenced by its neighbors, manages to sync its light emission with others. This results in entire groups of fireflies illuminating at once, a wonder of nature captured by understanding individual behavior and collective interaction.
Reduce the brightness of the light and watch the fireflies dance!
